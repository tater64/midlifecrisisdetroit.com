About

The Band

Midlife Crisis is Detroit's premier Faith No More tribute — bringing the full force of one of rock's most unpredictable and genre-defying acts back to the stage.

From the menacing funk of Epic to the cinematic darkness of Midlife Crisis to the chaos of The Real Thing, we go deep into the catalog — not just the hits.

If you've never seen FNM live, or you miss the hell out of them, this is the closest thing.
